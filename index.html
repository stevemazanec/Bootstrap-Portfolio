About Me

L.O.D.I.

LODI is an app used for local home contractors. A contractor can add information to the database. A customer can add reviews for a contractor. When a review is added, the contractor's overall average rating is updated. LODI uses MySQL, Node, Express, Handlebars, and a sequalized ORM. The app follows the MVC design pattern. Node and MySQL are to query and route data in the app. Handlebars is used to generate the HTML. Click on the picture to see the live site, or the title to see the code.
